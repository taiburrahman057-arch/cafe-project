Our Philosophy

Where Every Detail
Speaks Softly

"Upper Waste" is not simply a name — it is an aspiration. Immy founded this café with a singular belief: that extraordinary dining is born from restraint, honesty, and an obsessive attention to provenance.

Our kitchen sources locally, seasons simply, and lets the ingredient lead. From the first pour of your morning coffee to the final sliver of dark chocolate fondant — every moment here is curated with quiet care.
